    • TuLar

    TuLaR is an ongoing project being compiled within the CrossLingference project that intends to collect linguistic (lexical, morphological, and syntactical) and ethnographic data related to the Tupían languages. The data is made available under Attribution-ShareAlike 4.0 International (CC BY-SA 4.0). TuLaR comprises five databases all of which are work-in-progress in different stages of completion.
